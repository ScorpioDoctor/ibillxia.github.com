关于我

Jan 3rd, 2013 | Comments

I am a postgraduate student in Computer Science Department, Zhejiang University since Sep.6, 2012. My mainly research interest is Biometric via Voice Print Recognition(VPR). This is a research area related to machine learning, pattern recognition, etc. I also do some projects related to speech recognition.

我是浙大计算机学院2012级研究生，我的主要研究方向是基于声纹识别的生物认证， 声纹识别是一个与机器学习、模式识别相关的研究领域。有时，我也做一些语音识别相关的项目。
